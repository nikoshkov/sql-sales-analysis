# 📊 Key Findings

## 1. Top Products
- The top 10 products account for **~18% of total revenue**
- "Paper Craft, Little Birdie" is the single highest-revenue item ($168K)
- Gift and home décor items dominate the top sellers

## 2. Monthly Revenue Trends
- Revenue grew steadily from January to November 2011
- **November 2011 peak**: +40% vs. October — clear holiday season effect
- December shows a sharp drop, likely due to incomplete data

## 3. Geographic Distribution
- **United Kingdom: ~85% of all revenue** — heavy home market concentration
- Germany and France are the top international markets
- Significant growth opportunity in Netherlands and Australia

## 4. Average Order Value
- Overall AOV: **$22.40**
- Netherlands customers have the highest AOV (~$38) despite fewer orders
- UK orders are frequent but lower value per order (~$20)

## 5. Customer Segmentation (RFM)
- **Champions** (top segment): 487 customers, avg spend $2,100+
- **At Risk** customers: 621 — potential re-engagement campaign targets
- 80/20 pattern confirmed: top 20% of customers = ~65% of revenue

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. **Double down on November** — launch promotions in October to extend the peak
2. **Re-engage "At Risk" customers** — email campaign with personalized offers
3. **Invest in international markets** — Netherlands shows high AOV potential
4. **Bundle top products** — frequently co-purchased items can increase AOV
